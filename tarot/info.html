Y A T D

YATD is a tarot reading tool featuring an original 78-card pack , coded and illustrated by aloe lonely

the illustrations are done after the work of pamela colman smith, with great gratitude and thanks to her

YATD uses "true random" numbers derived from atmospheric noise provided by random.org , with delight and appreciation .

the diviniatory meanings and symbolic interpretations offered are excerpted from the pictoral key to the tarot , by arthur edward waite , with gratitude and thanks to him also

it must be noted that the pkt contains several interpretations clouded by misogyny , sexism and racism , and should be taken with a grain of salt , or its interpretations freely disposed of when necessary . passages have been included for their value as a starting-point and because the work is essentially linked with pamela colman smith's illustrations .

YATD is free - if you love it and want to make a donation , aloe accepts them here
