you are inside the little house

and you have approached the cactus

it's vaguely spherical , succulent with short spines , barely a foot tall in its plain ceramic pot . before you can bend over to take a better look , the cactus starts to hum ! faint at first but becoming unmistakable , a harmonious whirr emenatates from the otherwise unassuming plant .

as the hum spins up the cactus rises with its pitch through the very air , pot and all ! leveling out , it bobs comfortably in your eye line , sounding a pleasant little drone which modulates into a clear , if small , woman's voice .

once upon a time a witch lived here , watching the planets and doing her work . she leaves me behind to entertain curious travelers in her absence . welcome ! what would you like to do ?

looking around you do see a few bits of furniture which seem placed to invite your interest - a stack of cassette tapes beside their player on a table , an orderly bookshelf housing a disorderly collection of notebooks , a heavy leather-bound photo album and a door to the fenced-in backyard leaning slightly ajar . the cactus hovers and hums .
